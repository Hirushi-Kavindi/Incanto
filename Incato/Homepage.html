Our Story

INCANTO is a one-of-a-kind destination nestled beside the enchanting Sera Ella waterfall and natural cave in the Matale District of Sri Lanka, offering a harmonious blend of architectural elegance, cultural richness, and immersive experiences. Inspired by the traditional Tampita Viharaya design, the restaurant is built on stone with a waterproof foundation and wooden upper structure that blends seamlessly into its surroundings. Accessible via scenic eco-trails, INCANTO is positioned along popular tourist routes near Reverston and the Knuckles Range, making it a hidden yet reachable gem for nature lovers. The intimate 20-seat restaurant and café deliver a refined Italian-Sri Lankan fusion menu, artisanal beverages, and freshly baked goods, perfect for scenic brunches, romantic proposals, and stylish photo shoots. Guests can enjoy an exclusive 6-seat cave bar for curated wine experiences, or reserve a private cave for unforgettable moments. A boutique showcases Italian linen fashion and Matale’s handmade crafts, while cultural nights featuring live music, storytelling, and fusion performances bring warmth and connection to each visit. With its unmatched location, intimate ambiance, and thoughtfully curated services, INCANTO offers a truly sensory and unforgettable journey.
